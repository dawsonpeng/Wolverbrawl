## Overview

Wolverbrawl is a 3v3 multiplayer 2D pixel-art game inspired by Brawl Stars, themed around Harvard-Westlake. Players choose unique characters and compete in fast-paced, team-based game modes.

Platform: Laptop / PC

Controls: WASD or joystick

Art Style: 2D pixel art

Match Length: Short, high-energy matches (a few minutes each)

Heavy inspiration from Brawl Stars

Harvard-Westlake campus, culture, and faculty as thematic elements

Target Audience: Primarily students who enjoy competitive and casual multiplayer games

## Core Gameplay

3v3 multiplayer battles

Choose a character with unique abilities and stats

Fast rounds focused on teamwork and strategy

## Game Modes
Elimination
- 3 rounds per match. Each player has 3 lives. First team to lose all players is eliminated and loses.

Protect the Student
- Each team has a student taking a test. Opposing teams attempt to distract (attack) the other team’s student. Teams must defend their own student while disrupting the enemy’s.

More modes planned
